Volkswagen


It is a german motor vehicle manufacture headquater in wolfsburg , lower saxony ,Germany.
Founded in 1937 by the german labour front, known for their iconic Beetle ,It is the the flagship brand of the Volkswagengroup, largest car maker by worldwide sales in 2016 & 2017 .

The group's biggest market is inCHina, which delivers 40% of its sales& profit.

The German term Volk translate to "people",thus Volkswagen translate to "people's car".
